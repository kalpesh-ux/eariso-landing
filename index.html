About

Why Eariso exists.

I built Eariso because I wanted a space for honest self-observation that did not rush to fix, label, or advise.

Eariso was made for the moments when you do not need another answer — you need to see what is really driving the question.

Eariso exists to make hidden patterns visible. Quietly. Carefully. Without judgment.

Privacy

Your conversations stay yours.

Last updated: April 2026

Eariso is built for honest self-observation. Your privacy is central to that.

What we never do

  • train AI models on your conversations
  • sell your data to anyone
  • share your conversations with third parties
  • use your conversations for advertising
  • read your conversations manually

What we collect

When you create an account, we store your email address and login method — Google, Apple, or email. We do not ask for your name, location, age, or any other personal details beyond what is needed to identify your account.

When you use Eariso, your messages are processed in real time to generate responses. The system detects patterns from your conversations and stores:

  • pattern name
  • category
  • topic
  • one short quote from your own words
  • a one-or-two-sentence summary of what was emotionally present

This is what builds your Pattern Map.

What data we hold

Eariso holds only the following:

  • your email address
  • your login method
  • your detected pattern data — pattern name, category, topic, and one quote from your own words
  • a short summary of each conversation (one to two sentences)
  • your conversation transcripts — for 30 days only, then automatically deleted
  • your usage data, including conversation counts and subscription status

We do not store your full name, location, age, or payment details.

How your data is processed

Your messages are sent to a third-party AI provider for real-time processing. That provider does not store your conversations beyond short operational caching and does not use them to train their models.

Payments are handled by Stripe. We never see or store your card details.

Your rights

Eariso complies with UK GDPR. You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold about you at any time. When you delete your account, all associated personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

You can contact us at hello@eariso.ai to exercise any of your rights.

To request a copy of your data, email hello@eariso.ai. We will provide it in a machine-readable format within 30 days.

Deleting your data

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from your account settings. This permanently removes your pattern history, your account, and everything linked to it.

Data retention

We keep your conversations for 30 days. After that, only your patterns and short summaries remain. Your Pattern Map is unaffected.

If your account stays inactive for 12 months — no logins and no conversations — we will permanently delete your account and all associated data.

Children

Eariso is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. By creating an account, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. If we make significant changes, we will communicate them via email or in-app notification before they take effect.
